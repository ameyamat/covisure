Prevention from Corona Virus

stay home, stay safe

Know about covid-19

how to prevent virus

wash your place

Cleaning and disinfecting high-touch surfaces in your home regularly is an important precaution to lower the risk of infection. Follow cleaning product instructions for safe and effective use.

maintain distance

Avoid close contact with sick people. If possible, maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick & household members. If you are taking care of someone sick, make sure to wear a face mask.

don't touch face

To help prevent infections, keep your hands away from eyes, nose & mouth during the COVID-19 pandemic. Why? These mucous membranes are an entry portal for Covid-19 germs.

wash your hand

Make sure to wash hands after you blow your nose, sneeze into a tissue, use the restroom, before preparing or eating food, applying make-up, handling contact lenses etc.

use napkin

The Covid-19 virus is highly contaminable & hence it's necessary to use a paper napkin while sneezing, coughin etc. to help prevent spreading of germs.

wear a mask

Masks and face coverings can prevent the wearer from transmitting the COVID-19 virus to others and may provide some protection to the wearer.
